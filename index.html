About SINNKAWA®

Our brand story, philosophy, and culture

Our Story

Born in Singapore, SINNKAWA® is an original IP full of love and adventure. We hope to bring joy and warmth to people through cute characters and stories, while showcasing the charm of Singapore's multiculturalism.

Brand Milestones

Brand Foundation

The SINNKAWA® character was born, initially as a cute illustration.

2020

First Merchandise Release

Launched the first batch of SINNKAWA® merchandise, warmly welcomed by fans.

2021

First Store Opening

Opened the first SINNKAWA® themed store in Chinatown.

2022

Expansion to Haji Lane

Opened several specialty stores in the trendy Haji Lane district.

2023

Website Launch

The official website is about to launch, offering more online services.

2025

The Adventure Continues...

The SINNKAWA® family is growing! More fun IPs are coming soon. Stay tuned!
